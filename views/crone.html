Using crontab

Data scraping can be really useful. There are instances though where it would be useful to be able to observe that data through time but the source that you're scraping doesn't keep historical data kicking around. One solution is to scrape the source of data at regular intervals to make your own series. This of course comes with a bunch of it's own problems and it's no replacement for getting your hands on historic data i.e. you can only start building a series from today. 

      
alias crone="env VISUAL=nano crontab -e"
      
    

    
30 9 * * * sh ~/Desktop/projects/web/gitBackup.sh
